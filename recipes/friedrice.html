Fried Rice

This recipe is one that I have adapted from several different sources and establishments that I've frequented, it is by no means a creation of my own but rather a combination of many influences.

Fried can be an extremely easy thing to make provided you have a few tools and most importantly, rice, preferably, rice from yesterday's dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of fish sauce
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Let's begin by heating your choice of skillet or wok if you have one handy to med-high heat.
  2. Add the oil to the pan and once hot crack the egg directly into the oil and let it begin to set before stir-frying around the pan.
  3. Before the egg begins to brown or if it's runny enough to your liking, add your cup of cooked rice in and combine them well.
  4. While everything is stilling mingling in the pan, add your soy sauce and fish sauce, as well as the butter.
  5. Take a small taste and see if salt and or pepper are needed per your tastes.
  6. Remove from heat and serve. Enjoy!
